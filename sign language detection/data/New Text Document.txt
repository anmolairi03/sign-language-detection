Use your webcam or phone to record 10 short videos (5–10 seconds each) for each gesture.
Record 5 videos of “hello” and 5 of “thank you.”
Save them in a folder structure like this:

data/
  hello/
    hello_1.mp4
    hello_2.mp4
    ...
  thankyou/
    thankyou_1.mp4
    thankyou_2.mp4
    ...
